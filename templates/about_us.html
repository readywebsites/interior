About us

At Vastukar Architects, our integrated prowess spans architecture, real estate development, and commercial general contracting. This fusion of innovation, represented by Vastukar Architects, The Vastukar Development Group, and Competent Constructions, propels us to sculpt, construct, and nurture projects alongside investors and visionary clients who champion pioneering design philosophies. Our commitment revolves around pushing boundaries, embracing collaboration, and celebrating progressive concepts.

Elevating Architecture: Redefining Design Aesthetics Through Crafted Construction.

Vastukar Architects is a dynamic entity comprising architects, interior designers, and experts in real estate development. Our journey commenced in 2003, with a fervor to reshape living experiences. The synergy of Vastukar Architects, The Vastukar Development Group, and Competent Constructions empowers us to conceptualize, construct, and nurture projects. Our partnership extends to visionary clients who share our zeal for pioneering design and engaging interior spaces.

Our ethos transcends conventional architectural and interior design paradigms. The collaborative forces of Vastukar Architects, The Vastukar Development Group, and Competent Constructions stand as a testament to our innovation. We extend our reach across cities, from Gujarat to Maharashtra, Mumbai to Surat, Ahmedabad to Pune, Nashik and beyond, pushing boundaries to create living spaces that seamlessly marry cutting-edge architecture with transformative interior design.

Urban landscapes inspire our multidisciplinary team to fashion designs that respect community essence. Our journey, rooted in Southern Gujarat, has taken us to Surat and beyond, including Mumbai, Pune, and Nashik. Vastukar Architects, alongside The Vastukar Development Group and Competent Constructions, strives to create imaginative designs, not only architecturally but also within interior spaces, that resonate with their surroundings.

From architectureto interior design Since our inception in 2003, we've embraced a holistic approach to real estate development, an approach that integrates architecture, construction, and interior design. Our evolution, starting in 2003, continued with our establishment as a licensed general contracting entity in 2010, and further blossomed into Competent Constructions in 2016. Our comprehensive approach infuses value, innovation, and excellence, creating spaces that seamlessly harmonize both form and function.

The Team

Our approach, rooted in architecture, interior design, real estate development, and construction, orchestrates transformative spaces. Team collaboration ensures projects capture clients' aspirations while enhancing efficiency through streamlined communication. Urban revitalization remains our focal point, enriching neighborhoods with care, empathy, creativity, and innovation that are as evident indoors as they are outdoors.

Krunal Patil, co-founder of Vastukar Architects, is an architect and interior designer of remarkable vision. His journey, marked by experiences across Gujarat, Maharashtra, Mumbai, Surat, Ahmedabad, Pune, Nashik, and beyond, sets the tone for innovative architecture and interior design.

Since our establishment in 2003, Krunal has been a driving force, shaping designs that transcend aesthetics to enhance living experiences.
