Vastukar Interiors

Interior Design

Vastukar interior is a full service interior design service led by architects Krunal & Artei Patil, that focuses on mutli-use, ecofriendly, vastushastra-oriented commercial,retail and residential projects. Licensed and experienced in multiple states, Vastukar Interiors has a keen understanding of aesthetics and design, generating happy and condusive growth oriented environments, for business owners and families.

Vastukar offers consulting services in interior design, architecture, project management and urban designs. Founded on the principles of progressive and thought-provoking design, Vastukar believes that designing beautiful spaces is an art that all families and business owners should have the opportunity to experience.
